Features

Everything you need to turn your voice into text. It runs right on your computer, so your data stays private and safe.

Choose Your Model

Pick from different AI models to find what works for you. You can choose to make it super fast or super accurate.

Types For You

Just talk, and the app will type your words right into whatever you're using. You can even watch the words show up as you speak.

Copy and Paste

Don't want it to type for you? You can just have it copy your words so you can quickly paste them wherever you want.

Any Mic, Any Language

Pick which microphone you want to use, and speak in your own language. It works with lots of different languages from around the world.

Smart Punctuation

The app is smart enough to add periods and commas where they belong, so your text is ready to send right away.

Knows When You Stop

You decide how long the app should wait after you stop talking before it finishes the sentence. Work at your own pace.
